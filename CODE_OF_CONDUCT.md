# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on the technical merits of ideas.
- Assume good faith in discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Violations may result in removal from the community.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and extends to external communication when representing the project.
